Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ "" | date("MMMM d, yyyy") }}

1. Introduction

Diaeta ("Diaeta", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it and how we keep it safe. We process your data in strict compliance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and the Belgian Act of 30 July 2018 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data.

3. What personal data do we collect?

  • Identification & contact data: name, address, e-mail, phone, date of birth.
  • Health & dietary data (special category): medical history, diagnoses, allergies, lifestyle information, body measurements, personalised meal plans.
  • Financial & transactional data: invoices, payment status (card details are processed by our payment provider and never stored by us).
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information and cookie identifiers when you visit our website.

5. How long do we keep your data?

  • Client health file: kept for 30 years after your last consultation (professional & legal requirement).
  • Accounting records: 7 years (Belgian tax law).
  • Website technical logs: 6–12 months.
  • Enquiry data (non-clients): deleted after 12 months.

6. Who can access your data?

Your information is confidential. We only share it with:

  • Authorised staff bound by medical confidentiality.
  • Service providers (IT hosting, cloud storage, appointment scheduling, payment processing) under signed Data Processing Agreements.
  • Other healthcare professionals – only with your explicit consent.
  • Public authorities when required by law.

7. International transfers

Your data is principally stored in the EU. If a provider is located outside the European Economic Area, we rely on European Commission adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses to safeguard the transfer.

8. Security measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS, TLS-enabled e-mail).
  • Strong passwords & multi-factor authentication.
  • Regular software updates & security patches.
  • Encrypted backups stored within the EU.
  • Formal access-control list for health data (Belgian Act Art. 9).

9. Your rights

You may exercise the following rights free of charge:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Erase data in certain cases
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact us at info@diaeta.be. We will respond within one month.

10. Complaints

If you believe we are not respecting your data-protection rights, you may lodge a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority (APD / GBA): Rue de la Presse 35, 1000 Brussels, contact@apd-gba.be, dataprotectionauthority.be.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available on our website.
