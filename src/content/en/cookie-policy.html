Cookie Policy

Last updated: {{ "" | date("MMMM d, yyyy") }}

1. Introduction

Diaeta ("Diaeta", "we", "our", "us") operates the website diaeta.be. This policy explains what cookies are, which cookies we set, why we use them and how you can manage or withdraw your consent. Because our site deals with nutrition and health topics, certain cookies (e.g. analytics cookies that track which articles you read) may indirectly reveal information about your health. Under Article 9 GDPR such information is considered a special category of personal data. We therefore only set analytics or marketing cookies after we have obtained your explicit consent.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit it. They often contain a unique identifier and can serve various purposes such as remembering language preferences (first-party cookies) or tracking browsing behaviour for statistics or advertising (third-party cookies). Technologies that perform a similar function—e.g. HTML5 storage, local storage, tracking pixels and device fingerprinting—are treated as cookies by EU law.

3. Cookie categories

  • Strictly necessary cookies – essential for the operation and security of the website or to provide a service you explicitly request. They do not require consent (Art. 5(3) ePrivacy Directive).
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g. which articles are most read). They require your explicit consent.
  • Marketing / Social-media cookies – set by advertising or social-media platforms to build a profile of your interests or to measure ad performance. They also require your explicit consent.

5. Legal basis for processing

  • Strictly necessary cookies – processed pursuant to Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (our legitimate interest in providing a functional, secure website) and Art. 5(3) ePrivacy Directive.
  • Analytics & marketing cookies – processed only on the basis of your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) & Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR).

6. Managing or withdrawing consent

You can give or refuse consent via the cookie banner when you first visit the site. You can also change or withdraw your consent at any time by clicking the floating Cookie Settings link in the bottom-left corner of every page. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing that took place before withdrawal.

For further control you can delete cookies via your browser settings. See allaboutcookies.org for instructions.

7. International transfers

We host Matomo on a server located in the European Union. If you play an embedded YouTube video, data may be transferred to Google LLC in the United States. Google relies on the EU–US Data-Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses to safeguard such transfers.

8. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to request access, rectification or erasure of your personal data, to restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. You may withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@diaeta.be.

If you believe your data has been processed unlawfully you may lodge a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority (Autorité de protection des données / Gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit): autoriteprotectiondonnees.be.
